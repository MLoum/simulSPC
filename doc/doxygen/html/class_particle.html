◆ light_matter_interraction()

void Particle::light_matter_interraction ( )

Called at each tick of the main clock for each particle in order to know this particle has emitted a photon that is detected by the microscope.

There are four steps. First get the electrical field at the position of the particle (via the calculated PSF). Second calculate the mean number of absorbed and then reemited photon during one tick of the experiment Third, Based on the particle position and its angular orientation and its emission pattern, calculate (via the CEF) the probability that an eventual emitted photon is effectively collected by the microscope Fourth, Knowing the mean probability of counting a photon, use poissonian statistics to see if it is the case.
